DEV_LOG_003
TITLE: THE MAP IS NOT THE TERRITORY
STATUS: PUBLIC_BREADCRUMB

A clean workflow map can lie.

The real process may still depend on memory, side messages, and one person who knows which column nobody is allowed to touch.

The fix is not to insult the old system.
The old system probably carried the business longer than it should have.

The fix is to respect what it was trying to do, then replace the fragile parts with structure.

Names.
States.
Rules.
Logs.
Ownership.
Failure paths.

A system should not need folklore to explain how it works.

END LOG

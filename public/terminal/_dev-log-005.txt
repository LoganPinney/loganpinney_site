TITLE: EVERY GAME NEEDS A PROTOCOL
STATUS: PUBLIC_BREADCRUMB

The lab started as loose experiments.
Loose experiments are fine until they become a system.

Now each entry gets a protocol.

A protocol answers four questions:
what is it
where does it live
how do you launch it
what operational idea is hiding under the joke

That makes the archive maintainable.
It also makes the games feel intentional.

Games can be weird.
The registry cannot be vague.

END LOG
